03 : About Me

My Story

I am a student of technology on a journey to learn a variety of computer skills to prepare for the many job opportunities in the computer industry. I use online courses to try and give myself a comprehensive education in computer science and technology-related topics that I'm interested in.

My passion for learning about technology is mostly due to my lack of a computer science degree. I studied criminology at the University of Ottawa. During my time there, I discovered and developed an interest in computer programming. I taught myself the basics of some programming languages and over time I developed an interest in the various areas where they were applied.

Because I missed out on studying computer science as an undergrad, I try to learn what I can about programming, software tools, and computer science using online learning resources like Udemy. My endeavor to become skilled with computers comes from my desire to become adept in the technical subjects I should have learned years ago.
